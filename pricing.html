Waybill from one state to the other cost N1,000 but due to intra-state movement to the park, waybill may cost N1,500 some times.
Remittance occurs same day if the customer pays by transfer. If the customer pays by cash remittance will be the following day.
We will only be chanced to deposit by noon except you wouldn’t mind charges for pos.
Sometimes, POS charge in Ekiti is as much as N30 per N1,000 Sending items to states where we are to deliver makes job faster. However, we will need waybill it too.
