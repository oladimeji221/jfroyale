2018

In 2018, our journey began with the vision of our founder, Mr. Oluwsseun Akeju. Starting as a small enterprise, Mr. Joseph laid the foundation for what would become JFRoyale Logistics. With limited resources and a determined spirit, he initiated operations from a from a small shop that could barely accommodate 3 people, working tirelessly as the sole employee. Within a month, recognizing the growing demand for our services, he welcomed two new team members who would later become integral parts of our organizationâ€”the business manager and logistics head. Back then, we were handling an average of approximately 100 orders per month.

2019

The year 2019 marked a significant milestone in our growth story. We made a strategic move from our small shop to a larger and more accommodating space, a decision that allowed us to scale our operations effectively. With this expansion, we also expanded our client base, including online merchants who sought our services for their delivery needs. Our commitment to excellence led to a substantial increase in our monthly order volume, averaging at least 300 orders per month.

2020

While we were on a trajectory of continuous growth, the year 2020 posed unforeseen challenges with the global outbreak of COVID-19. The pandemic had a temporary slowdown effect on our operations as we navigated the evolving landscape, prioritizing the safety of our team members and customers. Despite these challenges, our resilience and adaptability allowed us to continue serving our valued clients to the best of our abilities.

2021

In 2021, we rebounded from the pandemic's impact, demonstrating our agility and commitment to our customers. We diversified our service offerings, introducing innovative solutions and streamlining our operations to enhance efficiency. Our client base continued to expand, and we solidified our position as a trusted logistics partner for businesses and individuals across Southwestern Nigeria.

2022

2022 saw further growth and development for JFRoyale Logistics. We expanded our reach and presence across multiple locations in Southwestern Nigeria, extending our logistics network to provide even more comprehensive services. With a team of over 20 dedicated experts, we continued to offer a wide array of solutions, including local and interstate package deliveries, specialized bus delivery options, corporate delivery solutions, efficient warehousing facilities, and e-commerce advisory and training services. Our commitment to excellence was recognized through our continued membership in the esteemed Delivery Agents Association of Nigeria (DAAN).

2023 till present

As we stand on the threshold of 2023, JFRoyale Logistics remains dedicated to providing efficient and reliable logistics solutions to our valued clients. Our journey from a small startup in 2018 to a thriving logistics company with a broad service portfolio is a testament to our unwavering commitment to promptness, efficiency, and unwavering integrity. We are proud to be a registered business under the Courier Regulatory Department (CRD) of the Nigerian Postal Service (NIPOST) and a member of DAAN, representing the interests of delivery agents across Nigeria. Our partnership with reputable financial institutions underscores our position as a major logistics player in Nigeria.

Thank you for joining us on this remarkable journey, and we look forward to continuing to serve you with dedication and excellence in the years to come.
